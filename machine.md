🧪 Age of Chemical - Machine Guide

Age of Chemical is an industrial expansion for UtilityCraft focused on biofuels, petrochemicals, automation, and advanced manufacturing. Every machine is designed to be part of a production chain, encouraging players to build complete factories instead of isolated machines.

---

🌱 Fermentor

The Fermentor is the first machine in the Age of Chemical progression. It converts crops and other organic materials into industrial fluids used throughout the addon.

Functions

- Produces Ethanol from sugar-rich crops.
- Produces Biomass from organic waste.
- Supports many vanilla and future custom crops.
- Fully automatable with pipes.

Example Inputs

- Sugar Cane
- Apples
- Sweet Berries
- Beetroot
- Potatoes
- Wheat
- Corn (Future)
- Sugar Beet (Future)

Outputs

- Ethanol
- Biomass

Ethanol is the primary ingredient for Biofuel, while Biomass is used in fertilizer production and future biological recipes.

---

🌿 Plant Oil Extractor

The Plant Oil Extractor processes seeds and oil-rich crops into Plant Oil.

Functions

- Extracts Plant Oil from seeds.
- Supports future custom crops.
- High efficiency compared to manual crafting.

Example Inputs

- Pumpkin Seeds
- Melon Seeds
- Beetroot Seeds
- Soybeans (Future)
- Canola (Future)

Output

- Plant Oil

Plant Oil is the second main ingredient required for Biofuel production.

---

🛢 Fuel Mixer

The Fuel Mixer combines Ethanol and Plant Oil into Biofuel.

Functions

- Mixes two different fluids.
- Stores both fluids internally.
- Automatically outputs Biofuel.

Recipe

Ethanol + Plant Oil → Biofuel

Biofuel is cleaner than crude fuels and is the starting point of the petrochemical industry.

---

⚗ Chemical Reactor

The Chemical Reactor converts Biofuel into Hydrocarbon Slurry using heat and catalysts.

Functions

- Performs advanced chemical reactions.
- Converts renewable Biofuel into industrial hydrocarbons.
- Supports future chemical recipes.

Recipe

Biofuel → Hydrocarbon Slurry

Hydrocarbon Slurry is the first petroleum-like fluid produced in the addon.

---

🧬 Separator

The Separator breaks Hydrocarbon Slurry into multiple useful fluids.

Unlike normal machines, the Separator has two fluid outputs, making it one of the first machines that requires advanced fluid management.

Outputs

Hydrocarbon Blend

A refined hydrocarbon mixture used for crude oil production.

Lithium Solution

A lithium-rich liquid used to obtain Lithium Dust for batteries, catalysts, and future energy technology.

The Separator encourages players to build proper fluid networks since both outputs are valuable.

---

⚙ Chemical Processor

The Chemical Processor further refines Hydrocarbon Blend into Crude Oil.

Functions

- Purifies hydrocarbon mixtures.
- Produces stable Crude Oil.
- Supports future advanced refining recipes.

Recipe

Hydrocarbon Blend → Crude Oil

Crude Oil is one of the most important resources in the addon and unlocks the refinery stage.

---

🛢 Refinery

The Refinery is one of the largest and most important machines in Age of Chemical.

It processes Crude Oil into multiple industrial products, each with its own production chain.

Outputs

Diesel

High-efficiency fuel used by Industrial Fuel Generators.

Petrol

A lightweight fuel used in advanced chemical processing and future recipes.

Naphtha

A petrochemical feedstock used to manufacture plastics, synthetic rubber, and other advanced materials.

Why It Matters

The Refinery marks the transition from renewable biofuels to a full petrochemical industry. Instead of producing a single output, it splits Crude Oil into several valuable products, encouraging players to build dedicated processing lines for each fluid.
